Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 24, 2026

The short version

Delivery Empire is a single-player, offline game. We do not collect, store or transmit any personal information. There are no user accounts, no analytics, no advertising, no trackers, and no third-party SDKs sending data off your device.

1. Information we collect

None. The app does not collect personal data. We do not ask for your name, email, phone number, contacts, location, photos, microphone, camera, or any similar information.

2. Information stored on your device

The app stores your game progress — such as your company name, cash, reputation, fleet, staff, contracts and upgrades — locally on your device using the operating system's standard storage mechanism (for example, UserDefaults on iOS and SharedPreferences on Android). This data never leaves your device and is not shared with us or anyone else. Deleting the app removes this data.

3. Internet access

The game does not require an internet connection to play and does not make network requests as part of gameplay. The operating system and the app store (Apple App Store, Google Play) may independently contact their own servers for purposes such as app delivery, updates and crash reporting; those activities are governed by their respective privacy policies.

4. Third-party services

We do not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, attribution, social login or tracking SDKs. No third parties receive information about you through the app.

5. Children's privacy

Because we collect no personal information from anyone, we collect no personal information from children. The app is suitable for general audiences. If you believe your child has somehow provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will confirm there is nothing to delete.

6. Your rights

Because we do not hold any personal data about you, there is nothing for us to access, correct, export or delete. You can remove all locally-stored game data at any time by deleting the app or by using the in-game "reset" option if available.

7. Data security

Game data is stored by the operating system in its standard app sandbox. We do not transmit your data anywhere, so there is no server or database on our side that could be breached.

8. International users

The app is intended for a global audience. Because no personal data is collected, there are no international data transfers to disclose.

9. Changes to this policy

If our data practices ever change — for example, if we add optional cloud saves or an opt-in analytics feature in the future — we will update this page and, where required, prompt you in the app before anything new begins. The "Last updated" date above always reflects the current version.

10. Contact

Questions about this policy? Email ertansoftwaresolutions@gmail.com or visit the Support page.
