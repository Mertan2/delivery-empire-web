Support

Need help, found a bug, or have an idea for the game? We'd love to hear from you.

Get in touch

The fastest way to reach us is by email: ertansoftwaresolutions@gmail.com. We read every message and aim to reply within a few business days.

Before you email

  • Please include your device model and OS version (for example, "iPhone 15 Pro, iOS 18.4").
  • Include the app version, shown on the in-game settings screen.
  • If reporting a bug, describe what you were doing and what happened. Screenshots help a lot.

Frequently asked

Does the game work offline?

Yes. Delivery Empire is fully offline — no account, no network connection needed to play.

Where is my save stored?

On your device only. Uninstalling the app deletes your save.

Can I transfer my save to another device?

Not currently — saves are local-only. If cloud save support is added in a future update, it will be announced in-app and on this page.

Are there ads or in-app purchases?

No. The game has no advertising and no in-app purchases today.

How do I reset my progress?

Use the reset option in the game's settings, or uninstall and reinstall the app.
